Here is my top 5, hope you'll enjoy !

1. How to be human by Ruby Wax

Buy it

This is a great book about the evolution of humankind. This book makes us aware about why humans act this way or another. It deals with different topics such as science, psychology, emotions, addiction or thoughts. This book is in a way, very spiritual.

2. Becoming by Michelle Obama

Buy it

A book about one of the most hardworking and successful women of the world. If you’re searching for some inspiration, this book might be the one for you. This book is a biography of Michelle Obama. This book is really inspiring, because she talks about her path and how she has succeeded in life.

3. Murder on the Orient Express by Agatha Christie

Buy it

If you like crime novel, I highly recommend this book for you. This is one of the most classical British novels of all the time. Hercule Poirot, a very well know character created by Agatha Christie is a detective who has to solve a murder which took place in a train. Suspense and mystery, while reading this book, you’ll not be bored.

4. To kill a Mocking bird by Harper lee

Buy it

A classic of American literature, this one is really moving. The scene takes place in the town of Maycomb in Alabama, suffering through the Great Depression. Atticus, a father of two children and a lawyer who hopelessly struggles to prove the innocence of a black man unfairly accused of rape.

5. The Alchemist by Paulo Coelho

Buy it

This is one of the greatest philosophical books of all the time. This story, incredible in its great simplicity, this book is full of wisdom and philosophy.This is about an Andalusian boy named Santiago who travels from his homeland in Spain to the Egyptian desert in search of a treasure buried near the Pyramids. He decides to embark on a journey to follow his dream.
